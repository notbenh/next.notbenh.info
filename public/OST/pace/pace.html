Idea for pace formula

The table below has been generated assuming that each contract is 24 $total_weeks and the course is 12 $total_lessons. The formula is as follows:

$pace = int(($lessons_complete/$total_lessons)*10) - int(($weeks/$total_weeks)*10)

Grades of pace:

Rushing:
$pace > 4
Ahead:
$pace > 1
On Pace:
1 >= $pace >= -1
Behind:
-1 > $pace
Urgent:
-4 > $pace
